My Neighborhood - Mason, Ohio

Mason is the largest city in Warren County, which is near the southwest corner of Ohio. Warren County is “Ohio’s Largest Playground” and Mason is the anchor. Come to Mason and stay for the fun! Regional attractions include The Grizzly Golf and Social Lodge, Mason Municipal Aquatic Center, Great Wolf Lodge and Conference Center, Kings Island amusement park, and the annual Western & Southern Open. Local attractions include the Mason Community Center and Mason Historical Society’s Alverta Green Museum. Residents and visitors look forward to the annual Red, Rhythm and Boom event, Mason Heritage Festival and Parade, Christmas in Mason activities and a wide variety of programs and classes for all ages at Mason Community Center.
